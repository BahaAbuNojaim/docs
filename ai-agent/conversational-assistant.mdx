## How AI Communication Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Describe Your Idea">
    Tell the AI your trading idea in plain English: "I want to buy Apple when it drops to $200."
  </Step>

  <Step title="AI Asks Questions">
    The AI asks clarifying questions about position size, risk management, and timing to complete your strategy.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm Your Strategy">
    Review the final strategy in clear language and confirm before activation.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Communication Tips

<Steps>
  <Step title="Be Specific">
    "Buy AAPL at $150" is clearer than "Buy Apple when it's cheap." Include exact prices and quantities.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Mention Risk Tolerance">
    "I want moderate risk with 2% position sizing" helps AI suggest appropriate parameters.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Include Timeframes">
    "Use 14-day RSI" or "Hold for 30 days maximum" provides the AI with clear constraints.
  </Step>
</Steps>
