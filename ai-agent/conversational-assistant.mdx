## Communication Tips

<Steps>
  <Step title="Be Specific">
    "Buy AAPL at $150" is better than "Buy Apple when it's cheap." Include exact prices and quantities.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Mention Risk Tolerance">
    "I want moderate risk with 2% position sizing" helps AI suggest appropriate safety measures.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Include Timeframes">
    "Use 14-day RSI" or "Hold for 30 days maximum" gives AI clear parameters to work with.
  </Step>
</Steps>
