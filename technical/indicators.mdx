## Key Technical Indicators

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Moving Averages" icon="chart-line">
    Show price trends by smoothing out daily noise. Golden Cross (50-day above 200-day) signals major moves.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="RSI" icon="activity">
    Measures overbought/oversold conditions on 0-100 scale. Below 30 = oversold, above 70 = overbought.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="MACD" icon="trending-up">
    Shows momentum changes through moving average relationships. Bullish when MACD crosses above signal line.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Bollinger Bands" icon="expand">
    Dynamic support and resistance based on volatility. Prices touching bands often signal reversals.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How to Use Indicators

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose Your Indicators">
    Start with 2-3 complementary indicators: moving averages for trend, RSI for momentum, and volume for confirmation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Clear Rules">
    Define specific conditions: "Buy when 20-day MA crosses above 50-day MA AND RSI is below 60."
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test Your Combinations">
    Backtest different indicator combinations to find what works best for your trading style and market conditions.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Popular Indicator Combinations

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Triple Confirmation" icon="check-circle">
    "Buy when price above 50-day MA, RSI below 60, and MACD turns bullish." Higher accuracy with fewer trades.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Trend + Momentum" icon="chart-mixed">
    "Buy on moving average crossover with volume confirmation." Classic trend-following approach.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Mean Reversion" icon="refresh">
    "Buy when RSI below 30 and price touches lower Bollinger Band." Works well in sideways markets.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Breakout System" icon="trending-up">
    "Buy when price breaks above Bollinger Bands with 2x volume." Captures explosive moves early.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Common Indicator Settings

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Moving Averages" icon="chart-line">
    20/50/200-day for different timeframes. Golden Cross: 50 above 200 signals major trend change.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="RSI Settings" icon="activity">
    14-period standard. 30/70 levels for normal markets, 20/80 for trending markets.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="MACD Parameters" icon="trending-up">
    12/26/9 standard settings. Focus on signal line crossovers and histogram direction.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Market-Specific Applications

<Steps>
  <Step title="Trending Markets">
    Use moving averages, MACD, and ADX. Focus on trend-following indicators with longer periods.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ranging Markets">
    RSI, Stochastic, and Bollinger Bands work best. Look for overbought/oversold conditions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Volatile Markets">
    ATR for risk management, Bollinger Bands for dynamic levels, and volume indicators for confirmation.
  </Step>
</Steps>
