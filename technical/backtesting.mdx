## How Professional Backtesting Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Historical Data Testing">
    Test your strategy on 3-5 years of real market data to see how it would have performed across different market conditions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Realistic Cost Inclusion">
    Include all trading costs: commissions, slippage, and market impact for accurate return calculations.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Performance Analysis">
    Review key metrics: total returns, win rate, maximum drawdown, and risk-adjusted performance.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Key Performance Metrics

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Total Returns" icon="chart-line">

  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Maximum Drawdown" icon="arrow-down">

  <Card title="Sharpe Ratio" icon="balance-scale">

## Understanding Your Results

<Steps>
  <Step title="Good Strategy Indicators">
    Look for win rates above 55%, consistent returns across different years, and drawdowns under 20%.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Red Flags to Avoid">
    Avoid strategies that only work in one market type, have huge drawdowns, or rely on very few trades.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Market Condition Analysis">
    Ensure your strategy performs reasonably well in bull markets, bear markets, and sideways periods.
  </Step>
</Steps>
