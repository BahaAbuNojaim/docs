## Risk Management Framework

<Steps>
  <Step title="Position Sizing">
    Limit exposure per trade. Adjust based on volatility and correlations between positions.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Portfolio Heat">
    Set thresholds for total risk across positions. Reduce exposure during highly volatile periods.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Exit Planning">
    Define reward-to-risk ratios and use trailing stops or time-based exits to manage trades.
  </Step>
</Steps>
