## Risk Management Framework

<Steps>
  <Step title="Position Sizing">
    Never risk more than 1% of portfolio per trade. Adjust based on volatility and correlation between positions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Portfolio Heat">
    Limit total risk across all positions to 6%. Automatically reduce exposure during volatile periods.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Profit Targets">
    Target at least 3x risk-reward ratio. Use trailing stops to capture extended moves.
  </Step>
</Steps>
