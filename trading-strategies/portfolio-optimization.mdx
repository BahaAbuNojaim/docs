## How Portfolio Optimization Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Asset Selection">
    Choose investments that complement each other - technology for growth, utilities for stability, international for diversity.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Risk Balancing">
    Distribute capital so each holding contributes appropriately to total portfolio risk and return.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Automatic Rebalancing">
    Maintain optimal allocations through systematic rebalancing based on your chosen schedule or thresholds.
  </Step>
</Steps>
