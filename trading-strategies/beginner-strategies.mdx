## Key Beginner Strategies

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Buy the Dip" icon="arrow-trend-down">
    Buy quality stocks when they drop below their average price. Often used by long-term investors seeking systematic entry points.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Momentum Breakout" icon="chart-line">
    Enter positions when stocks break above recent highs with strong volume. Designed to capture potential upward price moves.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Dividend Collector" icon="coins">
    Buy dividend-paying stocks when temporarily undervalued. Designed to provide exposure to both dividends and price appreciation.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Risk Management" icon="shield">
    Apply stop-loss and position sizing rules to help manage downside exposure.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
